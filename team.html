Founder & CEO

Connor

Connor is the founder of CTekAI, a done-for-you AI automation agency built specifically for trades businesses across the US. After seeing how many service businesses were losing jobs simply because they couldn't answer every call, he set out to build the systems that fix it — permanently.

Operating remotely, Connor designs and deploys AI voice agents, automation workflows, and custom web apps that let roofers, HVAC companies, plumbers, and electricians compete on the same level as businesses three times their size. His approach is straightforward: understand the business first, build only what moves the needle, and make sure it actually runs.

He runs every part of CTekAI himself — from the first discovery call through to go-live and ongoing support. No account managers. No handoffs. You work directly with the person building your system.

CTekAI is his conviction that the trades industry is ready for AI — and that the operators who move first will win.
